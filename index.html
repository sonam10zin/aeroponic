What is AEROPONIC?

Aeroponis is the process of cultivating plants in an air or mist environment, eliminating the need for soil or an aggregate medium. The term "aeroponic" orginates from the Greekwords aer ("air") and ponos ("work").

How does Aeroponics grow?

Aeroponics is a method of growing plants without soil. Instead roots are suspended in the air and irrigated with a nutrient-dense mist. This differs from hydroponics, where plant roots are submerged in a solition of water and nutrients.

Why choose aeroponic?

Higher growth rates create a quicker turnover of your product.

Greater control of nutrient application enables you to consistently tune crop quallity.

Because mist is applied only to the root zone, the growing medium remains dry, so there is less chance of losing profit to pests or disease.
